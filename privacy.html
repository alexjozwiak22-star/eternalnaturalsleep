Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how EternalNaturalSleep ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our content publishing tool and TikTok integration.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process personal data from any users or third parties. Our application operates solely on behalf of a single owned TikTok creator account (@EternalNaturalSleep) for the purpose of uploading pre-produced video content.

2. TikTok Integration

Our application uses the TikTok Content Posting API exclusively to publish short-form video content to our own TikTok account. We access only the permissions required to post video content (video.publish). We do not access, read, or store any data from other TikTok users or accounts.

3. Data Storage

No personal data is stored on our servers or local systems beyond the OAuth access token required to authenticate with the TikTok API on behalf of our own account. This token is stored locally on our private machine and is never shared.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party APIs solely for content production purposes:

  • ElevenLabs — text-to-speech voice generation
  • Pexels — royalty-free background video footage
  • TikTok Content Posting API — automated video publishing

No user data is shared with any of these services.

5. Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our TikTok account @EternalNaturalSleep.
